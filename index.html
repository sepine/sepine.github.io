I am now a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Xiapu Luo. I received Master and Bachelor degrees in Software Engineering from Wuhan University in 2022 and Hubei University in 2019, respectively.

My research interests lie in designing security systems and improving software quality with the help of program analysis, data mining, machine learning and deep learning techniques. Currently, I mainly focus on program analysis, blockchain and smart contract security.

Research Interests

Blockchain and Smart Contract Security; Binary Analysis; Software Engineering
