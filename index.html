Biography

I am a student at School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, China. I am also a visiting student at Key Laboratory of Dependable Service Computing in Cyber Physical Society, Ministry of Education, Chongqing University. My research interests include software engineering, deep learning, and natural language processing.
