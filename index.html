Biography

I am Kunsong Zhao, a Master student at School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, China. My research interests include software engineering, data mining, and natural language processing. The close cooperative relations have been established with well-known universities at home and abroad, such as Chongqing University, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, City University of Hong Kong, Macau University of Science and Technology, Wuhan University of Technology, Monash University (Australia), Tilburg University (The Netherlands), Syracuse University (USA), and et al.
