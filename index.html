I am a student at School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, China.

My current research lies in how to improve software quality and save the effort of developers during software development by mining informative data in software repositories, and how to bridge the linguistic gap between different monolingual knowledge graphs via natural language processing techniques. In addition, I am planning to explore other related topics by code modeling.

Research Interests

Intelligent Software Engineering; Natural Language Processing
